About the Composer

Maxim Novak is an independent composer creating melodic, nostalgic instrumental music for film, video, and podcasts. His style blends retro aesthetics with jazz, bossa nova, and elements of country — always with a cinematic feel and emotional depth.

Over the past few years, Maxim’s music has been featured in several documentary films and used by content creators across platforms. His credits include music for the feature-length film Ties (2024). He is also listed on IMDb as a composer.

Maxim works directly with filmmakers and storytellers, offering original music and carefully curated instrumental collections. His work is available for licensing and collaboration.
